> EXAMPLE rule. Replace the rule canon with your own operating rules.

After every significant piece of work, give a comprehensive summary. Never just "done".

**Content:**
- Which files were created/changed + why
- How the pieces fit together
- How to use/run what was built
- What the owner needs to know going forward
- Caveats, footguns, safety nets

**Why:** the owner needs full visibility. Otherwise they have to ask. A terse "done" forces a round-trip.
